MOONDROP MOCA - Designed for Ease of Use and Convenience

Enhance your listening experience with the Moondrop MOCA, a masterpiece of engineering and elegance created for both audiophiles and lifestyle aficionados. Successor to the classic TWS NEKOCAKE, The TWS MOCA combines cutting-edge technology with minimalist design to provide unrivaled audio performance and seamless simplicity.

Unparalleled Sound Quality

Powered by a TiN ceramic dome diaphragm dynamic driver, the MOCA brings your music to life with astonishing clarity, powerful bass, and precision. Whether you're listening to your favorite songs or going deep into podcasts, every detail is delivered flawlessly.

Transparent Transmission Mode

The MOCA adapts to your environment using auto-transparency and photo-sensor wear detection. When worn, it dynamically changes to transparency mode, ensuring that you are always aware of ambient sounds for safety and situational awareness when it is most important.

Hassle-Free Wireless Charging

No more fumbling with cables. Simply place the side with the wireless charging symbol on the charging device. The MOCA charging case is compatible with Qi-certified chargers, making it a convenient solution for your modern lifestyle.
